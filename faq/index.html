Frequently Asked Questions

Using SpaceX - Launch Tracker

What is SpaceX - Launch Tracker?

SpaceX - Launch Tracker is an unofficial SpaceX app powered by the r/SpaceX API providing detailed launch information.

Why can't I get it on iOS?

I don't currently plan on created an iOS due to time constraints. However you should be able to find other apps that have similar functions.

Privacy & Data Usage

Do you collect any personal data?

No! The application does not collect any personal data.

So what exactly do you collect?

We use Google Crashlytics to collect error reports. This makes it easier to identify and fix bugs for future releases. Crashlytics provides us with your phones model and crash details.
